About ENSTA

Click to expand.

Welcome to The National Higher School Of Advanced Technologies. This University was established in 2022 after the merger of the National Higher School of Technology and the Higher School of Applied Sciences of Algiers, bringing together their staff, assets and expertise.

It is a public scientific, cultural and professional institution with legal and financial autonomy. It ensures higher education, scientific research and technological development in fields such as industrial engineering, transport, automation, electronics, telecommunications, mechanics, electrotechnics and process engineering. Missions

ENSTA goal is to train engineers who not only master scientific and technical skills but also know how to apply them creatively to solve real problems. The school encourages research, entrepreneurship and collaboration with industries helping students turn their ideas into innovations. It also promotes a spirit of curiosity, teamwork and continuous learning to prepare graduates for the challenges of a rapidly changing world.
